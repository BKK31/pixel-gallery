Pixel Gallery is a sleek, privacy-focused gallery application designed to provide a premium user experience. It leverages the power of Material You dynamic theming to adapt to your device's wallpaper, ensuring a seamless and personalized look for your photo collection.
From managing your albums to viewing motion photos and map locations, Pixel Gallery creates a beautiful and private home for your memories.

Key Features:
• Privacy First: All data and processing stay on your device.
• Material 3 Design: Modern, expressive UI with dynamic color support.
• Motion Photos: Full support for viewing and managing motion photos.
• Map Integration: View your photo locations using OpenStreetMap.
• Favorites & Recycle Bin: Easily organize your best shots and recover deleted items.
• Fast & Fluid: High-performance image loading and smooth transitions.
